Our History

MALANGAS
Zamboanga Sibugay

Founded on vibrant civic pride and blessed with rich natural resources, Malangas stands as a testament to the beauty of Mindanao. Originally known for its rich coal deposits, the municipality has blossomed into an agricultural and ecological haven.

Our local governance is deeply rooted in transparency, community welfare, and sustainable development — preserving our lush emerald landscapes for generations to come.
